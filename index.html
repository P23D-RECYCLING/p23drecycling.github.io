##Catch the plastic before it gets into the landfill

Plastics No. 1, 2, & 5

A commitment to special handle the disposal of #1,2 and 5 identifiers and putting it into a recycle bin with a red lid. The red lid would go to a special recycle facility that converts it into 3D material. In essence it is about a movement to change the way people look at the overwhelming amount of waste we have and need to be aware of and stopping it from going into the oceans or landfill.
